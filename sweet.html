hi there

Seven true things
about me

no pressure. i just figured you should know what you might be getting into.

One

trivia nights

I keep an alarming amount of useless knowledge in my head and almost none of the useful kind. I light up at bar trivia. I'll pick a team name that's a pun, and I'll be quietly thrilled if you laugh at it.

Two

seaweed

I think seaweed is underrated and I will gently, persistently try to convince you. The roasted kind, the salad kind, the kind floating in a good broth. It's a small thing, but I get delighted by small things.

Three

wine-drunk karaoke

I cannot sing. I mean it. But give me a glass of wine and a microphone and I become fearless in the most embarrassing, joyful way. The duet slot is open, if you're brave.

Four

graphs

This is a real one: I love a good graph. A clean trend line makes me happy in a way I can't fully justify. I notice patterns everywhere. I'd probably make a little chart about you, and I mean that as the highest compliment.

Five

stray cats

I cannot walk past a stray cat without stopping. I crouch, I do the little sound, I let them decide. Most of them ignore me. I love them anyway. I think you can tell a lot about a person by that.

Six

horror games

I play scary games despite being genuinely, pathetically easy to scare. I'll jump, I'll yelp, I'll make you hold the controller during the worst part. It's more fun with someone next to you, honestly.

Seven

a good soup

And this is the truest one. I think a good soup is a love language. If I like you, I'll make you soup — that's the softest, most sincere thing I know how to offer. So consider this the whole page building toward a single, honest sentence:

that's me. seven true things.
if any of them made you smile, i'd love to hear which one.
