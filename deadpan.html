A Preliminary Compatibility Analysis

A peer-reviewed1 case for allocating time to one another.

Abstract. The subject (hereafter "me") presents seven independent variables of questionable social value. We hypothesize that, when observed together, they constitute a person worth getting a drink with. Preliminary findings are promising. Sample size: 1. Confidence: alarmingly high.
